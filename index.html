What I Do

My name is JASON ANDREW. I've worked as a professional Forex and Futures trader And Binary Options for over 16 years, both privately and in major firms across the USA.

When I started I was a single dad at age 19 in 2009. Today I have been blessed to help over 15K students, we have created 73 millionaires. Many students find success with us. We have put on over 25 live events. I will continue to do this because it’s much bigger than me, it’s about helping YOU and there have been over a dozen people who have worked with me and started their own things teaching others and the cycle repeats.Even though ways in teaching may differ and no one way is ever 100%, it’s a beautiful thing to share knowledge. I wish you all success and happiness.

Most online trading courses encourage you to start risking real money from day one, but institutional training takes a different approach, professionals aren’t trusted with live funds until they’ve proven consistent profitability, often after a year or more. After recognizing this gap, I decided to create my own course, built on real industry standards. Drawing from years of experience, I’ve designed a program that teaches trading the right way, from foundational concepts to advanced strategies, so that anyone, not just those in firms, can access a proper trading education.
